Welcome to Smart Campus

Manage classrooms, labs, equipment and services efficiently. Smart Campus RMS centralizes bookings, tracks availability, and reduces double-bookings so faculty, staff and students can focus on what matters.

Explore Resources Create Account

Previous approaches

Traditionally, resource management relied on paper schedules, email threads and siloed spreadsheets. This caused lost requests, manual conflict resolution and no single source of truth.

What we solve

Smart Campus automates bookings, prevents overlaps with conflict checks, stores a searchable history of usage, and offers one place to request rooms, equipment and services.

Benefits

Faster approvals, better utilization of space and equipment, transparent audit trails, and analytics that help campus planners make informed decisions.

How it works (quick)

  1. Sign up and sign in to access advanced actions.
  2. Browse resources and check live status.
  3. Request or reserve resources for specific time slots (demo limited).
  4. Admins can approve or manage requests centrally.
